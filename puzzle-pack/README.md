# Puzzle Pack

Versioned puzzle-pack JSON exports and checksums are generated here by the
deterministic content pipeline.

- `season-preview.json` is the 20-day preview pack used for feature work.
- `season-one-candidate.json` is the Phase 14 full-season candidate scaffold:
  365 days and 1,825 puzzle records. It is not release-ready until human review
  and repeated-content replacement are complete.
