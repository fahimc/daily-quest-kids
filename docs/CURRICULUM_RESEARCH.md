# Curriculum Research

Sources checked on 2026-07-19:

- GOV.UK national curriculum overview for Key Stage 2 ages and assessments.
- GOV.UK English programmes of study for lower and upper Key Stage 2.
- GOV.UK English Appendix 1 spelling guidance and statutory word lists.
- GOV.UK mathematics programmes of study for Key Stage 2.
- GOV.UK science and geography primary programmes of study.

Audience:

- Ages 7-10.
- Years 3-5 as the core audience.
- Early Year 6 content only for challenge days.

Vocabulary Guidance

- Years 3-4: familiar school, family, nature, home, weather, sport, story and
  simple science/geography words.
- Years 4-5: broader reading vocabulary, common prefixes/suffixes, homophones,
  word families and morphology.
- Years 5-6 challenge: statutory-list words, less common affixes and more
  precise clue vocabulary.

Word and Sentence Length

- Wordly: five-letter British English words only.
- Spelling B: three-letter minimum; seven-letter all-letter word where practical.
- Crossword answers: three-letter minimum, mostly 3-7 letters.
- Clues: usually 5-12 words for starter/core, 8-16 words for challenge.
- Explanations: one short sentence unless a hint needs a second simple sentence.

Suitable Clue Complexity

- Starter: direct definition or concrete example.
- Explorer: simple synonym, category clue or sentence with context.
- Thinker: light wordplay, morphology or curriculum-linked general knowledge.
- Challenge: precise vocabulary, fair red herrings and early Year 6 concepts.

Challenge-Only Words

Words from the statutory Years 5-6 spelling list, uncommon homophones,
multi-morpheme words and abstract terms should be challenge-only unless their
meaning is heavily supported by clues.

Topics to Use

- Nature, plants, animals, materials, weather, space basics.
- UK places, maps, rivers, mountains and simple fieldwork vocabulary.
- Number patterns, shapes, times tables, fractions, symmetry and logic.
- Reading comprehension, word families, prefixes, suffixes and homophones.

Topics to Avoid

- Profanity, sexual content, drugs, gambling, graphic violence, frightening
  imagery, current politics, celebrity gossip, current news, brands and public
  usernames.

Learning Support by Puzzle Type

- Wordly supports spelling, phonics, morphology and vocabulary precision.
- Spelling B supports letter patterns, word families and flexible word building.
- Crossword supports clue comprehension, vocabulary recall and spelling.
- Sudoku supports logical reasoning, elimination and mathematical fluency.
- Connections supports categorisation, semantic reasoning and vocabulary links.

Difficulty Progression

- 35% accessible Years 3-4.
- 50% core Years 4-5.
- 15% challenge Years 5-6.
- No more than two challenge days consecutively.
