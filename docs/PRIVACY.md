# Privacy

Daily Quest Kids is designed for children and must be private by default.

Current policy:

- No internet permission.
- No adverts.
- No analytics or tracking SDKs.
- No accounts, login, leaderboard, chat or online social features.
- Puzzle packs, progress, settings, streaks and achievements are local.
- Shared images must omit answers, personal identifiers, device identifiers,
  location and metadata.

Parent-facing summary:

The game provides offline daily puzzle practice. Progress stays on the device.
The Android Sharesheet can be used for locally generated result pictures, and
children are reminded to ask a grown-up before sharing outside the app.
